How to Activate Your JCPenney Credit Card?
=========================================

To activate your JCPenney Credit Card, follow these steps:

- Visit `jcpcreditcard.com <https://www.jcpcreditcard.com>`_ using your phone or computer.
- Click on **“Register / Activate”** or **“Activate Card.”**
- Enter your credit card number, ZIP code, and SSN (last 4 digits).
- Create a user ID and password for your online account.
- Verify your identity by following the on-screen instructions.
- Submit the details to complete activation.

Your JCPenney Credit Card is now activated and ready to use.

Alternate Method (By Phone)
---------------------------

- Call the activation number printed on the sticker on your card.
- Follow the automated voice prompts.
- Enter your card details when asked.
- Wait for confirmation of activation.
